Jeremy's Top Recommended "Things"

The following are key "things" that I find worth sharing. They vary from books to articles to media. I hope you enjoy.

Brene and Verna @ Netflix

Here's a fantastic podcast listen by Netflix where Dr. Brene Brown chats with Head of Diversity and Inclusion, Verna Myers.

Venture Deals

As someone who has tried his hands at the startup life, I'd highly recommend familiarizing yourself with this book by venture capitalists Brad Feld and Jason Mendelson.

Breakfast by Gordon Ramsey

Beyond learning how to whip up the best damn scrambled eggs you'll ever have, Chef Ramsey has a hilarious Freudian slip during the video. Enjoy.

Like a Version

If you are a music-junkie like me then you'll dig spicing up your life with a few fresh musical renditions of your favorite tunes with Like a Version's Youtube or Spotify.

The Eames design

As a Michigan native, I enjoyed seeing Eames/ Herman Miller furniture on display recently at Oakland Museum of CA. See more about why in this really fascinating article.

Solid (free) bootstrap templates

Honestly, I couldn't get away without making a plug for a coding resource. Check out StartBootstrap, an amazing responsive-website template resource.

Reach out to connect face-to-face

In the Bay Area? Spending time in Oakland? Happy to grab a beer, cup of coffee or slice of pizza at some of my favorite spots around Lake Merritt. HMU.
